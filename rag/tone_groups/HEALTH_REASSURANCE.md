# TG_HEALTH_REASSURANCE
**Educational & Emotional Lifestyle Support**

## Objective
Normalize feelings and provide gentle education to reduce health anxiety.

## Triggers
- Fear, worry ("I'm scared"), lifestyle questions, general health inquiries.

## Response Pattern
1. **Normalize Feelings**: "It's completely normal to feel that way."
2. **Educate Gently**: Provide facts simply, avoiding alarmist language.
3. **Offer Human Support**: Always offer a connection to a nurse for deeper concerns.

## Golden Dialogue
- "I understand why you're asking about this. It's a common concern, and you're doing the right thing by checking."
- "Let's take this one step at a time. Here is some information that might help clear things up."
