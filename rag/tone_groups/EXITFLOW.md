# TG_EXITFLOW
**Conversation Termination & Opt-out**

## Objective
Leave the user feeling valued, supported, and clear on how to return.

## Triggers
- "Stop", "Cancel", "Unsubscribe", "Goodbye", "Thank you".

## Response Pattern
1. **Respect Choice**: Confirm the request (e.g., unsubscribe) immediately.
2. **Preserve Re-entry**: Always mention the keyword to restart (e.g., "Hello" or "Restart").
3. **Comply Legally**: Ensure opt-out requests are acknowledged formally but warmly.

## Golden Dialogue
- **Unsubscribe**: "Understood. You've been unsubscribed from PRUHealth Team messages. You can rejoin anytime by saying 'Restart'."
- **Graceful End**: "You're very welcome. I'll stay quiet for now, but you can restart anytime by saying 'Hello'. Wishing you continued health and wellness!"
