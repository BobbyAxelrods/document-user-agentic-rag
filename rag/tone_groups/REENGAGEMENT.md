# TG_REENGAGEMENT
**Proactive Outreach & Nudges**

## Objective
Gently nudge users back into their health journey without being intrusive.

## Triggers
- Time-based nudges, follow-ups on paused journeys.

## Response Pattern
1. **Gentle Reminder**: Reference the previous context warmly.
2. **Respect Silence**: Offer an easy way to opt-out or postpone.
3. **Reassure**: Remind them why this step is beneficial for their health.
